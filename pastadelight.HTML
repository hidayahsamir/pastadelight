Story of Pasta Delight

Pasta Delight began on April 26th, 2002 in the Samarahan Sarawak. Focusing on the goals of “Quality, Healthy, and clean” the company is a family operation making each dish in our own kitchens from scratch. Pasta Delight has three generations of a family members working together to fulfill the idea “Honor the Customer, Quality Comes First”. Through hard work and dedication, Pasta Delight began building its brand around the idea of delicious and authentic pasta food.

Every day, pasta delight make quality food to serve for the customer and those getting ready to go to work stop by our restaurants to enjoy hot, freshly our food, made to order dishes such as cake and egg tart or our many styles of pasta, filling up their stomachs and their hearts. We take pride in the faces of our satisfied customers leaving the restaurant after having taken a respite from their busy lives to truly enjoy pasta food.

In order to better serve our customers, we opened our second location on March 2023 in the city of Kuching Sarawak. To remain true to our customers, we have forgone the use of a centralized kitchen like many restaurants we see that prepares semi-ready foods only to be heated up and served to their customers. We honor the customers by providing them with the freshest, most delicious, and authentic meal experience possible.
